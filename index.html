The Strategy

How value is built.

  1. 01

    Acquire

    Buy land where public records—future land-use maps, infrastructure plans, highway projects—already point growth. The path is drawn before it’s priced.

  2. 02

    Generate Income

    Lease land for grazing, hay, and hunting while you hold. Lease income covers taxes and operations. The land pays its own way while value compounds.

  3. 03

    Entitle

    Pursue rezoning and development approvals. This is where value is manufactured—the legal step that unlocks development potential and re-rates land multiples.

    Entitlement risk is real, priced, and disclosed in the deal memo.

  4. 04

    Exit at Peak

    Sell entitled land at your partnership’s defined exit window, when development value is highest. Partners get capital back first, then share gains.

Illustrative mechanics — not a projection.

Why This Works

Cities grow along maps you can read.

Cities expand along documented corridors. Future land-use maps, utility boundaries, and highway projects are public records. You can see where growth is headed before capital prices it in.

Income carries the hold.

Agricultural leases cover taxes, insurance, and management costs. Your capital isn’t bleeding while you wait. The land earns its own way.

Entitlement compounds value.

Zoning approval is the largest lever in land valuation. We pursue it actively, not hope for it. That legal step unlocks multiples of gain.

Alignment

We win when you win.

Our compensation is tied to your returns. The managing directors take no annual fees and earn carry only after partners are repaid—if the land underperforms, they aren’t paid either.
