The Strategy

Land. Income. Entitlement. Exit.

  1. 01

    Buy

    Parcels in the path of metro growth, selected where the county’s own future land-use map already points.

  2. 02

    Carry

    Grazing, hay, and hunting leases target covering taxes and upkeep — the land pays to wait, and agricultural use keeps the tax basis low.

  3. 03

    Entitle

    We pursue rezoning and approvals — the step where land is legally cleared for development and value re-rates.

    Entitlement can be denied. That risk is priced and disclosed in the deal memo.

  4. 04

    Exit

    Entitled parcels are sold at the partnership’s defined window, and proceeds are distributed to partners first.

Illustrative mechanics — not a projection.

Why Land

Cities grow along maps you can read.

Future land-use plans, utility boundaries, and highway projects are public documents. The path of growth is drawn before it is priced.

Income turns waiting into holding.

Land that pays its own taxes can wait out a slow decade. Land that bleeds cannot.

Entitlement is manufactured, not hoped for.

The largest value step in land is a legal one. We do that work rather than waiting for someone else to.
